About Me
Justin Kisner

Hello! My name is Justin and I am 24! Welcome to my "About Me" page!

I am from a rural town in Eighty Four, Pennslyvania. I grow up with a love of the outdoors. From hunting and fishing, to playing sports, I try to find time outside as much as possible. More recently, deer hunting has been the only time I can get out and disconnect, by myself and just enoying the outdoors. I look forward to the season every year and possibly of loosing time to go out haunts me! I also have two dogs and two cats! Their names are Bear and Kugo, and JD and KitKat.

I also am learning farsi in my free time. This has been a difficult, but fun way test myself and connect more with my best friend.

Professionally, I am a jewelery salesperson at Macy`s. I have worked there for three years and plan to work there until graduation. Once I graduate, I do not have a "dream job" in mind. I am looking for something I can grow in and find enjoyment doing. I choose CIS to find a steady job in a growing field, it is not my passion, but is an interest that I do enjoy and wish to learn more and more about.
